I come from an Electrical Engineering background — but somewhere along the way, I got pulled deeper into the data side of things. The more I worked with systems, the more I realized the real interesting problems were in the data that drove them.

That curiosity led me to teach myself Python, statistics, and ML from scratch — and eventually land an ML & Data Engineering internship at a startup, where I now work on real AI products end to end.
